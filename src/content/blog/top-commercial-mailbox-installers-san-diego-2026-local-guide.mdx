## Compliance Variations Across San Diego County

Each city in San Diego County has specific requirements. Here's what installers need to know:

**San Diego**: Permits required for new mailbox installations or relocations. Historic district properties need additional design review. Estimated permitting: 5–10 days.

**Carlsbad**: Fast permitting (3–5 days) but strict aesthetic standards. Mailbox finishes and placement must align with community character guidelines.

**Chula Vista**: Streamlined permitting for standard installations. Electrical connections require separate permits if parcel lockers are powered.

**Escondido**: Permits required; rural and suburban properties may have different requirements. ADA accessibility is strictly enforced.

**La Mesa**: Standard permitting process. Hillside properties may require geotechnical review if ground-mounted CBUs are planned.
